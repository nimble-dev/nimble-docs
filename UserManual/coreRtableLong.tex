  \hline
  Function & Comments (differences from R) \\
\cd{c()} & no \cd{recursive} argument \\
\cd{rep()} & no \cd{rep.int} or \cd{rep\_len} \\
\cd{seq()} and \cd{:} & Negative integer sequences from \cd{:}, e.g. \cd{2:1}, do not work. \\
\cd{which()} & No \cd{arr.ind} or \cd{useNames} arguments. \\
\cd{diag()} & Works in three ways: diag(vector) returns a matrix; diag(matrix) returns the diagonal vector; diag(integer) returns an identity matrix.  No \cd{nrow} or \cd{ncol} arguments.\\
\cd{diag()}<- & Works for assigning the diagonal vector of a matrix.\\
\cd{dim()} & Works on a vector as well as high-dimensional arguments. \\
